# DragonMind Craft — Gameplay Guide

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| WASD | Move |
| Space | Jump |
| Shift | Sprint |
| Left Click (hold) | Break block |
| Right Click | Place block |
| E | Open inventory |
| 1-9 | Select hotbar slot |
| Scroll | Change hotbar slot |
| Esc | Pause menu |
| F11 | Fullscreen |
| F12 | Dev tools |

## Survival Basics

### Health & Hunger
- You have 20 HP (10 hearts)
- Hunger depletes as you move and act
- Below 18 hunger: no regeneration
- At 0 hunger: starvation damage
- Eat food to restore hunger

### Mining
- Left-click and hold to mine blocks
- Different tools mine different blocks faster
- Stone needs a pickaxe, wood needs an axe
- Bedrock cannot be mined

### Crafting
- Press E to open inventory (with 2x2 crafting)
- Right-click a Crafting Table for full 3x3 crafting
- Key recipes:
  - Oak Log → 4 Oak Planks
  - 2 Oak Planks (vertical) → 4 Sticks
  - 3 Planks + 2 Sticks → Wooden Pickaxe
  - 8 Cobblestone → Furnace
  - 4 Planks → Crafting Table

## Progression

### Tool Tiers
1. **Wood** — Basic tools, mine stone and below
2. **Stone** — Mine iron ore
3. **Iron** — Mine gold and diamond ore
4. **Gold** — Fast but fragile
5. **Diamond** — Best vanilla tier
6. **Dragon** — DragonMind exclusive, strongest in game

### Finding Resources
- **Coal**: Common, found 0-128
- **Iron**: Found 0-64
- **Gold**: Found 0-32
- **Diamond**: Found 0-16 (rare)
- **Dragon Ore**: Found 0-25, only in Dragon Stone zones (ultra rare)

## Dragon Content (Unique to DragonMind Craft)

- **Dragon Stone**: Deep purple stone found deep underground
- **Dragon Ore**: Ultra-rare ore in Dragon Stone zones
- **Dragon Crystal**: Glowing purple crystals on mountain peaks
- **Dragon Scale Block**: Crafted from Dragon Scales, stronger than Obsidian
- **Dragon Sword**: 12 damage, 3000 durability
- **Dragon Pickaxe**: 8 damage, mines everything
- **Dragon Armor**: Maximum protection set

*Find Dragon Ore → Mine Dragon Scale Block → Combine with Dragon Heart for ultimate gear*

## Day/Night Cycle

- One full day = 20 minutes real time
- Dawn at 25%, Noon at 50%, Dusk at 75%, Midnight at 0%
- Hostile mobs spawn at night
- Passive mobs roam during day
- Torches provide light and prevent mob spawning nearby

## Saving

- World auto-saves every 5 minutes
- Manual save: Esc → Save World
- Worlds stored in browser localStorage
- Multiple world slots supported
